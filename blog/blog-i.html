food!

March 19th, 2021

This school year I got my first apartment, we’re still in quarantine, and my interest in food has only grown. As a result of this, I’ve started cooking… a lot. Usually, college students are known for being lazy when it comes to food, as we don’t have the extra money to spend and are usually busy doing other things. Since I’ve downloaded TikTok last year, making new recipes has given me a fun new hobby.

I have found 99% of my inspiration from this app, and it turns out, these people know what they’re doing! So, for this week’s blog, I’ve decided to make a list of my favorite recipes I’ve gotten from TikTok.
